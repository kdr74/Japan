Complete Hour-by-Hour Plans - All 17 Days

Complete Trip Overview

15 nights, 3 regions, unforgettable experiences

Phase 1: Tokyo (Days 2-5, 5-8 April)

Base: MIMARU Tokyo Ueno Okachimachi (4 nights)

  • Day 2: Arrival day - Ueno Park cherry blossoms, Asakusa Senso-ji Temple
  • Day 3: teamLab Borderless digital art (pre-book!), Odaiba waterfront, Rainbow Bridge sunset
  • Day 4: Akihabara anime/manga district, Meiji Shrine, Harajuku Takeshita Street, Shibuya Crossing
  • Day 5: Imperial Palace Gardens, Tokyo Station, Ginza shopping, Tokyo Tower, luggage forwarding

Phase 2: Mt Fuji (Days 6-7, 9-10 April)

Base: Habitacion Cottage Jin, Kawaguchiko (2 nights)

  • Day 6: Highway bus to Kawaguchiko, Lake exploration, Mt Fuji photography
  • Day 7: Chureito Pagoda sunrise, Mt Fuji Panoramic Ropeway, traditional onsen experience

Phase 3: Kyoto & Kansai (Days 8-14, 11-17 April)

Base: Tokyu Stay Kyoto Sakaiza (7 nights)

  • Day 8: Shinkansen to Kyoto (ACTIVATE JR PASS!), Fushimi Inari gates, Gion evening
  • Day 9: Kinkaku-ji Golden Pavilion, Ryoan-ji zen garden, Philosopher's Path cherry blossoms
  • Day 10: Universal Studios Japan FULL DAY with Express Pass 7 (pre-book!)
  • Day 11: Kiyomizu-dera wooden stage temple, Higashiyama traditional streets
  • Day 12: Arashiyama Bamboo Grove (go early!), Tenryu-ji Temple, Monkey Park
  • Day 13: Nara day trip - deer park, Todai-ji Great Buddha, Kasuga Taisha Shrine
  • Day 14: Epic three cities tour - Himeji Castle, Kobe beef lunch, Osaka Dotonbori (08:30-22:00)

Phase 4: Return (Day 15, 18 April)

Base: Villa Fontaine Grand Haneda Airport (1 night)

  • Day 15: Shinkansen back to Tokyo, final shopping/sightseeing, early night for 05:00 wake-up
