üèÆ Essential Tips & Culture

Onsen (Hot Springs) Rules

CRITICAL RULES:

  1. NAKED bathing only (gender-separated). NO swimsuits.
  2. WASH thoroughly BEFORE entering bath
  3. Tattoos often banned (cover small ones)
  4. Small towel - DO NOT put in bath water
  5. Hair tied up
  6. Quiet environment

Restaurant Etiquette

  • Wait to be seated
  • Say "itadakimasu" before eating
  • Never stick chopsticks upright in rice
  • Slurping noodles is GOOD
  • NO TIPPING - it's rude!
  • Pay at register, not at table

Temple & Shrine Basics

Shinto Shrines:

  1. Bow at torii gate
  2. Purify at water basin
  3. At main hall: Toss ¬•5, bow twice, clap twice, bow once (2-2-1)

General Behavior

  • Speak quietly in public
  • No phone calls on trains
  • Stand left on escalators
  • Carry rubbish (few public bins)
  • Queue properly

Money

  • Cash is king
  • 7-Eleven/FamilyMart ATMs work
  • Carry ¬•10,000-20,000 (¬£60-120)

Useful Phrases

  • Sumimasen: Excuse me / Sorry / Thank you
  • Arigatou gozaimasu: Thank you very much
  • Konnichiwa: Hello
  • Eigo ga hanasemasu ka?: Do you speak English?
